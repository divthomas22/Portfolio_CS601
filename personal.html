    Divya was born and raised in the Chicago suburbs. When she was younger, her dream was to become a neurologist, even going into her undergrad at University of Iowa as a pre-medicine student! After 2 years, though, she realized that her passion did not lie in medicine and she was more interested in expanding her analytical and problem-solving skills in software programming. This is what led her to her Bioinformatics bachelors degree. She has two younger brothers and is the proud owner of 4 adorable parakeets: Juno, Luna, Pika (short for Pikachu), and Rico.

    Iowa Agni

    Divya participated in a South Asian fusion Acappella team, Iowa Agni, all throughout her undergrad at the University of Iowa. Here, she took on the role of President her Sophomore to Senior year, while also filling in as music director/composer, creative director and choreographer on occasion. Divya displayed her exceptional leadership and team building skills as she built Agni from the ground up, brought great recognition campus-wide and even placing second at their first ever national competition in team history! Feel free to check out Iowa Agni on Instagram at @iowaagni!
